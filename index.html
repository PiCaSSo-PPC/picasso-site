PiCaSSO Digital Platform
The PiCaSSO team is developing a digital platform to improve Pediatric Palliative Care in Portugal. It will offer a clinical data registry, advanced analytics, and a user-friendly interface for healthcare professionals to track, analyze, and coordinate PPC for children with CCD. The platform will support care planning and network creation through secure, centralized data.
